About Us

Mission Statement: Connecting those seeking a career in the trades with the information and resources they need for a path to success.

Vision: Create a society where there are clear, well defined, and desirable career paths for individuals without a college degree.

Objectives:

  1. Provide a platform where skilled trade workers can be connected to individuals and organizations around them that need their services
  2. Connect young, aspiring skilled trade workers to apprenticeship programs or trade schools that best meet their individual needs
  3. Provide people with the information and resources they need to make the most informed decision about whether a career in the trades is right for them
  4. Destigmatize the attendance of trade schools and apprenticeship programs and promote trade work as a desirable career path
